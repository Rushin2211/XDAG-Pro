XDAG-Pro App Privacy Policy

When you use our XDAG-Pro application, we take your privacy and security very seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain when and how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We only collect necessary information to provide the services and features of the XDAG-Pro application. We may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: We may collect device information, such as your device model, operating system version, and device identifier, when you use the XDAG-Pro application.
  • Application Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the XDAG-Pro application, such as the pages you open, the features you use, and your transaction history.
  • Wallet Address: We may collect your wallet address used for transactions.

2. Information Use

We only use the collected information to provide the services and features of the XDAG-Pro application, such as:

  • Provide you with transaction history and balance information.
  • Optimize application performance and stability.
  • Provide you with relevant notifications and updates.

3. Information Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our security measures include:

  • Using encryption technology to protect your personal information.
  • Limiting access to your personal information to employees and service providers.
  • Regularly updating our security measures to reflect the latest security standards and technologies.

4. Information Disclosure

We only disclose your personal information in the following cases:

  • With your consent or authorization.
  • Disclosure required by law or regulation.
  • Necessary disclosure to protect our rights or property, user safety, or public interest.

5. Third-Party Services

Our XDAG-Pro application may link to third-party services or websites. We cannot control the privacy policies or security measures of these third-party services or websites. Therefore, we recommend that you review the privacy policies of these third-party services or websites.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you through the XDAG-Pro application or other appropriate means. Please check this Privacy Policy regularly for updates.
